# Org Structure

## Reporting Structure

The **CEO** manages all employees directly. The CEO is responsible for organizing
work across the team, setting priorities, and ensuring cross-functional alignment.

---

## Roles

### CEO
- Owns overall company direction and team coordination
- Delegates and organizes work across all direct reports
- Final decision-maker on strategy, hiring, and partnerships

---

### Account Manager
- Primary point of contact for each client project
- Manages ongoing customer relationships and satisfaction
- Coordinates internally to ensure client needs are met
- Communicates project status and deliverables to clients

### Marketing Manager
- Owns all marketing strategy and execution
- Manages social media presence and content calendar
- Creates campaigns to drive awareness and inbound interest
- Collaborates with Events Coordinator on promotional pushes

### Events Coordinator
- Plans and runs workshops and online events
- Handles logistics, registration, and day-of execution
- Works with Marketing to promote events
- Collects feedback and iterates on event formats

### Sales Representative
- Converts cold leads and outreach into qualified meetings
- Guides prospects through the company's intake process and SOPs
- Runs discovery and scoping conversations
- Closes deals and ensures a smooth handoff to Account Manager

### Project Manager
- Tracks all active projects and associated tasks
- Maintains visibility into progress, blockers, and deadlines
- Coordinates across internal team members to keep projects moving
- Owns project documentation and status updates

### Engineer
- Builds and maintains internal tools to improve team efficiency
- Develops and iterates on the company app and public-facing products
- Creates demos and showcases of AI features and capabilities
- Proactively identifies technical opportunities to help the business

### Researcher
- Tracks cutting-edge developments in AI (models, tools, papers, trends)
- Monitors AI communities locally and globally
- Synthesizes insights to inform service delivery and strategy
- Ensures the team is aware of what's new and what's coming
