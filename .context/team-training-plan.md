# OneDay Team AI Training Program

## Overview

A new service line: **hands-on AI training for small teams and companies**. This builds directly on our 1:1 sessions and workshops but packages them for groups of 3-15 people within a single organization. The key differentiator is that everything is customized to the company's actual tools, workflows, and pain points — not generic "intro to AI" content.

---

## The Product: "OneDay for Teams"

### Format

**Phase 1: Discovery & Prep (before the session)**
- 30-min call with the team leader/owner to map out:
  - What tools the team uses daily (Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, etc.)
  - Top 3-5 workflows that eat the most time
  - What the team has already tried with AI
  - Specific outcomes the leader wants (e.g., "I want my team to stop spending 3 hours on weekly reports")
- Short survey sent to each team member (5 min) to understand individual roles and pain points
- We build a **custom curriculum** based on this — not a generic slide deck

**Phase 2: Live Training Session(s)**
- **Option A — Half Day (3 hours):** One focused session. Best for teams that want a foundation.
- **Option B — Full Day (2 x 3-hour sessions):** Morning = foundations + hands-on building. Afternoon = advanced workflows + automation specific to their stack. Best for teams that want to walk out fully operational.
- Delivered on-site or via video call (Google Meet / Zoom)
- Every person builds something real during the session — a workflow, a tool, an automation — using their actual work data
- We cover the tools relevant to THEIR stack (not a generic tool tour)

**Phase 3: Follow-Up & Support (after the session)**
- Recap document with every prompt, workflow, and resource from the session
- 2 weeks of async support via Slack/email for questions as people start using AI day-to-day
- Optional: 30-min group check-in call 2 weeks after training to troubleshoot and reinforce habits

## How to Sell It

### Sales Process

1. **Warm intro / outreach** — For these three, you already have the relationship. Send a short, personal message (not a sales pitch). Something like:

   > "Hey [name] — I've been putting together a team training version of what I do with AI. Thought of you immediately because [specific reason]. Would love to do a quick 30-min call to see if it'd be a fit for your team. No pressure either way."

2. **Discovery call (30 min, free)** — This is where you learn their workflows, tools, and pain points. You're also qualifying: Do they have budget? Is the team willing to learn? What does success look like for them?

3. **Custom proposal (1 page)** — After discovery, send a short proposal:
   - "Here's what we heard"
   - "Here's what we'd cover"
   - "Here's the expected outcome"
   - "Here's the investment"
   - Include 1-2 relevant testimonials

4. **Close** — Follow up within 48 hours. Offer to lock in a date. For early customers, you can offer a "founding teams" rate (10-15% off) in exchange for a testimonial and case study.

| "We already use ChatGPT" | "That's great — it means your team is already curious. What we do is take them from casual prompting to building real systems: automations, agents, and tools that save hours every week. Most people use about 5% of what AI can do." |
